Zombie Types

Runners:

These zombies are faster than regular zombis, capable of chasing their prey for extended periods of time.


Weakness:

Low IQ.


Voodoo:

These zombies are controlled by a single magical person and/or item and can only be stopped when that person or item is destoryed.


Weakness:

Dependant on voodoo source.


Romero:

These are the classical zombies often portrayed in movies and/or some videogames.


Weakness:

No special abilities or ties.


Crawler:

These zombies stick low to the ground, often catching their prey by surprise.


Weakness:

Easy to kill from above.


Brute:

Brutes, sometimes called Bloaters (like in The Last of Us), are bigger and stronger than regular zombies In videogames, it often takes more time and strategy to take them out.


Weakness:

Slow and dumb.


Screamer:

Screamers, sometimes called Clickers (like in The Last of Us), use sound to track and even immobilize their prey.


Weakness:

Powerless if you can't hear them.
