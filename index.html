Mythical Monsters

Ghost

An apparition of a dead person which is believed to appear or become manifest to the living, typically as a nebulous image.

Vampire

A mythical creature that subsists by feeding on the vital essence (generally in the form of blood) of the living.

Zombies

Is a mythological undead corporeal revenant created through the reanimation of a corpse.

Siren

Are female humanlike beings with alluring voices; they appear in a scene in the Odyssey in which Odysseus saves his crew's lives.

Banshees

Is a female spirit in Irish folklore who heralds the death of a family member, usually by screaming, wailing, shrieking, or keening.
