More Information

Vampires:


These creatures of the night feast on the blood of humans with no mercy. If they seem freindly, do not believe it.

WARNING!!!:

Vampires cannot enter your home without your permission. DO NOT LET THEM IN



Werewolves


These hairy beasts only come out when the moon is full. Once they find their prey, they will stop at nothing to get it. Current known weaknesses are silver, wolfsbane, iron, and electricity.

WARNING!!!:

DO NOT LET THEM BITE YOU OR YOU WILL BECOME ONE FO THEM!!!



Ghosts


These white shadows roam the halls of haunted houses and mansions at night, often forcing out any intruders for their own personal reasons, wether that be revenge, fear, or boredom.

WARNING!!!:

Some ghosts may try to posses you. Best way to prevent this: DON'T TAUNT THEM!



Witches


These skilled spellcasters often reside in cottages, practicing their spells and brewing their potions, traditionally only coming out at the stroke of Midnight, also known as the Witching Hour.

WARNING!!!:

Do NOT approach. EVER.

Zombies


These rotting wanderers rise up from the ground to feast on flesh and brains. Those they do not eat are turned into one of them. Some believe that they will actually rise one day and humanity will have to come together to stop them from destroying civilization.

May not be as civil as the zombies protrayed in the movie series: Zombies.
